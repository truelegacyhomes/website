Why We Do This

When my own family went through a difficult transition, I saw firsthand how overwhelming it can be. The house. The belongings. The memories. The decisions that feel impossible to make when you're grieving.

I wished there had been someone to help—not just with the logistics, but with the emotional weight of it all. Someone who would treat my family's things with respect, and give us space to process while handling the details.

That's why I built True Legacy. We don't just manage estate sales or buy houses—we walk alongside families during some of the hardest chapters of their lives.

— Paul Williamson, Founder & CEO
