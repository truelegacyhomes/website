How do I know if an estate sale is the right option for my parent‚Äôs belongings?

What legal steps should I take before starting an estate sale?

Confirm your legal authority to act on behalf of the estate (executor, administrator, or trustee). Locate the will or trust, letters testamentary or administration, and any applicable death certificates. Start with a clear, written process: identify keepsakes, set a request deadline, and use fair tie‚Äëbreak rules (e.g., draft rounds, point budgets, or random draw).
