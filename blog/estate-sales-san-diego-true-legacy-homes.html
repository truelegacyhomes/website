FAQ

What are estate sales?


Estate sales typically occur when an older person has passed away or moved to a smaller home and their remaining assets need to be liquidated. Liquidation in the simplest terms refers to the conversion of hard assets to cash. … Liquidation of an estate most often occurs when someone in the family dies, and refers to the disposition of everything owned by that person (2)

The goal of hosting an estate sale is to clear the house of all belongings—usually in preparation to be sold—without the need to dispose of the contents directly. An estate sale is a huge undertaking and requires many weeks of prep before the event in addition to the labor of the sale itself. Because of the size and emotional toil of hosting an estate sale, many families choose to hire a company to organize, advertise, and manage the event on their behalf. This is where pTrue Legacy Homes comes in. As a team who understands the pain of transition, we’ve made it our goal to simplify the estate sale for San Diego families.

Our services are always tailored to the needs of our families, and we want nothing more than for you to be happy with the results of your sale.

Why do people have estate sales?


There are two primary reasons someone may choose to hold an estate sale. The first and most common reason is that someone has had a loved one pass away and they’ve inherited the house with their belongings still inside. When this happens, many people will host an estate sale to clear out the house and prepare it to be sold.

The other main reason someone might choose to host an estate sale is that they’re downsizing or relocating and need to liquidate the majority of their belongings in a short period of time. For example, they may be moving into a retirement community where they’ll have a smaller living space. This would be a great opportunity to host an estate sale in order to downsize before the move.

Regardless of the motivation, hosting an estate is always a complex and emotional process. And when you’re in a life transition or grieving the loss of someone close to you, managing an estate sale on your own can quickly become too much to handle. It’s at these times that having a team of estate sale professionals can make all the difference.
