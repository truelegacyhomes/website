2735 Morningside Street, SD, CA, 92139


Old and unusual, the aftermath of many Mexico and South America outings during the 50’s and 60’s, rare souvenirs of days gone by, clay figures, copper plates, photographs, post cards, costume jewelry, books and booklets, cast aluminum patio furniture and garden ornaments.

You will also find plenty of mid-century solid wood furniture, oak tables, Samsonite steel and wood arm chairs and nostalgia artifacts: typewriters (Smith-Corona Coronet), tools, lamps (including Tiffany style shades) and projectors (Argus 300) with lots of historical 35mm slides and photographs dating back to the 19th century, historical documents, newspapers and magazines.

Among the many relics in this house there is a vast collection of antique bottles, fossils, petrified wood, rare rocks and even a desiccated turtle; Avon collectibles, old books (an 1877 bible among them), potted plants, Halloween and Christmas ornaments.

Household items and kitchenware, a stove, refrigerator, washer and dryer, a large bird cage, lots of garden ornaments and a Twin Star International electric fireplace with wooden wall mantel.
