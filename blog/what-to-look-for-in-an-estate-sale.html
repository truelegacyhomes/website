What to Look for in an Estate Sale


Paul Williamson – February 24, 2023

It’s no wonder why people are excited about estate sales Chula Vista. There’s a lot to gain here once you are able to enter the scene, loads of items are there waiting for you to discover and snatch for yourself. An entire house filled with items will certainly carry special pieces and treasures, as this is a great opportunity for those who are on the hunt for special finds, vintage items, and even antiques.

The wait in line, the intensity of the auction, and the anticipation of what lies behind closed doors can be thrilling. Sure, it is not as easy as garage sales tend to be, but the reward is often greater. While you may have to dig through some junk in order to find the jewels, it can be done. Today we will uncover what are the main elements to look for when dealing with an estate sale and how to make some serious money out of it.

Before all else, you should learn as much as you can about the estate sale itself. Who is organizing? What kind of merchandise is for sale? How much are items being sold for? Is there a catalog or list of items available prior to auction day? All that matters and provides you an advantage over other buyers.

What Is An Estate Sale

An estate sale is a fascinating event that offers a chance to explore the possessions of someone who has passed away or is downsizing. From furniture to collectibles, antiques to clothing, estate sales offer an eclectic mix of items that can pique anyone’s curiosity. It’s a sale that can be both exciting and emotional, as it presents an opportunity to own a piece of someone’s past.

Estate sales in Chula Vista are held by specialized companies offering services for homeowners to quickly and equally distribute the possessions of their loved ones. Family members also organize it if they can manage all the hustle and bustle, or else they hire specialists to do the job for them. Items pricing varies depending on the auctioneer and their experience, but there are more factors to take into account.

Are you ready to find out what items would be worth spending your money on? Read on. Before we go into details it’s better to define some important terms that are used in estate sales

Antique: It is a piece that has been around for more than 100 years and generally looks a bit worn out due to age, however, they carry great value and are usually sought after by collectors.

Vintage: A furniture piece or an item at least 20 years old is called vintage.

Retro: Items produced in the 1950s are considered retro.

Tag Sale: Someone’s items available at a discounted price are usually called a tag sale.

Art Prints and Paintings

Paintings and art prints make beautiful additions but more often than not, they are more valuable when you know who’s behind them. Take pictures of the painting with your phone and search for its author online in case it does not come with a signature. Original artworks can be sold at an impressive price or kept as part of your own collection.

If you have a keen eye for oil paintings, sculptures, metalware, and other art you can make a fortune in an estate sale. Some buyers can easily spot a priceless item, estate sales Chula Vista CA are the perfect venue for them to make a great investment.

Vintage Furniture

Mid-century furniture is the most sought after and there are chances to find a pair of iconic chairs or an enameled dresser. The great news is that vintage furniture pieces often come in sets and can be used to revamp your home or office. Plus, you can always give them a fresh coat of paint if needed. Estate sales are one of the best places to find them; beautiful, solid wood furniture pieces from the past add a lot of charm and personality to any room.

Kitchenware

Kitchens are always a fun place to explore in an estate sale. Taking a closer look at the various kitchen utensils, appliances and gadgets is a great way to uncover some hidden gems while also stocking up on useful items that you can use in your own everyday cooking. From classic dinner sets to pottery, you will come across a lot of interesting finds. Vintage kitchenware is often used in photoshoots or venue decoration providing another source of income if you are into photography.

Books

Estate sales are known for bookcases filled with literature written by different authors, from many different eras. Non-fiction books, classic novels, and even a rare first edition can be found in the mix. It is always exciting to find a collectible book that has been well preserved over time. The best part of all? Most books sold in an estate sale are significantly cheaper than what you would pay in an online or physical store.

Jewelry

Jewelry is considered to be one of the most valuable items, there is just no comparison to the price one can get for gold, diamonds, and other precious stones. In estate sales, jewelry items are often found in great condition, from classic golden rings to unique statement pieces. It is worth mentioning that jewelry has one of the highest profit margins when it comes to reselling so you can really make a good deal by investing in this area.

It’s hard to find fine jewelry—gold or silver—at an estate sale because those items tend to sell privately before the sale. However, there is a lot of vintage costume jewelry that can be worth some serious cash. Look for signed pieces and those that are made with quality materials like crystal and glass. The more unusual the item, the more it could be worth. (1)

Porcelain or Vintage China

Porcelain and vintage china are now finding their way into European and American homes. They can vary from plates to teacups, making them great items for starting a collection or sprucing up your home decor with some vintage charm. Paying $1,000 for a brand-new set is costly. Why not consider some Chinese porcelain to enhance the beauty of your dining room?

Antiques

While talking about estate sales, it is impossible not to mention antiques. Antiques are one of the most valuable items one can find in an estate sale and their prices don’t remain the same over time. Let’s say you have found an old mirror or a restore-worthy cabinet; you can sell these items for a much higher price than what you paid in the estate sale.

Collectible Items

Collectible items such as dolls, figurines, or vintage toys can bring a sweet profit. If you are lucky enough to stumble upon these items in an estate sale, don’t hesitate to buy them. Even if you don’t have any plans to sell, these would make a perfect enjoyable thing for your kids or grandkids or keep as part of your own collection.

These are just some of the key things we outlined here, you will see many more interesting items in an estate sale. Although it isn’t a piece of cake to find an item worth thousands of dollars, there’s still a chance that you may benefit in a lot of ways from estate sales San Diego. So, keep your eyes peeled and check out estate sales regularly to get the best deals. Happy hunting.
