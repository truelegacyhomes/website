1540 Pedregal Dr Escondido, CA 92025


Fantastic Estate sale in sunny Escondido! Beautiful home full of fine arts, exceptional furniture and diverse collectibles. This house holds original oil paintings, wood carvings, old tapestry, wall decor and a wide array of collectibles from around the world. There’s a wide selection of vintage collectibles from the 80’s 70’s and 60’s. Including Tonka, Nylint, Buddy L, Barbie, Disney, Peanuts, McDonald’s restaurants and others. Old comic books, postcards, vintage greeting cards, magazines, menus, travel souvenirs and lunchboxes. You’ll also find exceptional furniture that is so hard to come by. Mid century pieces, hand painted Asian chests, vintage chairs and accessories throughout the house. Silver jewelry and tons of costume jewelry. Huge selection of crystal pieces, variety of Fenton glass, Exquisite glass paper weights, solid brass ornaments. Lladro, Hummel and Limoge pieces. Tons of dolls from around the world, books, vintage clothing for women including purses. Loads of kitchenware including fine pots and pans. A garage full of tools, toolboxes, a vintage Yamaha motorcycle with helmet, vintage and new bicycles. In the music section a room full of vinyl records, two flutes, a player piano with loads of rolls to play with and a fine harp. Definitely something for every one!
