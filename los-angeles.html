Los Angeles Estate Sale FAQs

How much does an estate sale cost in Los Angeles?
Most Los Angeles estate sale companies charge 30-40% commission. True Legacy Homes works on commission only—no upfront fees. You don't pay unless we sell.
How long does it take to prepare for an estate sale?
Typically 1-2 weeks. We handle all staging, pricing, and marketing so you don't have to lift a finger.
What items sell best at Los Angeles estate sales?
LA buyers love: mid-century modern furniture, Hollywood memorabilia, vintage fashion, designer items, fine art, collectibles, musical instruments, and high-end electronics.
Do you serve all of Los Angeles County?
Yes! We serve the entire LA metro area including the Westside, San Fernando Valley, San Gabriel Valley, South Bay, and everywhere in between. Call us to confirm service in your specific neighborhood.
What happens to items that don't sell?
We arrange donation pickup or disposal. Many LA clients donate remaining items to local charities. Your home will be left broom-clean and ready.
How do estate sales work for probate in Los Angeles?
We work with Los Angeles probate attorneys regularly. We can provide court-required documentation and fair market value appraisals for the probate process.
