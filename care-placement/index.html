What is Senior Care Placement?

It's a personalized service that helps families find the right assisted living, memory care, or board-and-care home based on medical needs, preferences, and budget.

Assisted Living

For seniors who need help with daily activities like bathing, dressing, and medication management, while maintaining independence.

Memory Care

Specialized care for those with Alzheimer's or dementia, with trained staff and secure environments designed for cognitive needs.

Board & Care

Smaller, more intimate residential settings with personalized attention and a home-like atmosphere.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really no cost for your services?

Correct. Our services are 100% free to families. We're paid by the care communities when a placement is made, allowing us to offer unbiased guidance at no charge to you.

How do you determine the best care option?

We do a full assessment of care needs, mobility, cognitive condition, financial situation, and location preferences—then match you with vetted communities that fit those criteria.

How quickly can you help us find a care home?

In urgent situations, placements can happen within 24–72 hours. We work at your pace, whether you're planning ahead or facing a time-sensitive move.

What types of communities do you work with?

We work with a wide network of licensed facilities including assisted living, memory care, and residential board-and-care homes—ensuring a range of options for every need and budget.

Can you help with the transition and estate sale too?

Absolutely. Many families need help with multiple services. We can coordinate care placement alongside an estate sale and even a cash offer on the home—making the entire transition seamless.
