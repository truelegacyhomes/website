Orange County Estate Sale FAQs

How much does an estate sale cost in Orange County?
Most Orange County estate sale companies charge 30-40% commission. True Legacy Homes works on commission only—no upfront fees. You don't pay unless we sell.
How long does it take to prepare for an estate sale?
Typically 1-2 weeks. We handle all staging, pricing, and marketing so you don't have to.
What items sell best at Orange County estate sales?
OC buyers love: mid-century modern furniture, coastal decor, designer items, fine jewelry, art, luxury goods, and high-end outdoor furniture.
Do I need to be present during the estate sale?
No. Most clients prefer to be away during the sale. We handle everything and provide detailed reporting.
What happens to items that don't sell?
We can arrange donation pickup or disposal. Many Orange County clients donate remaining items to Salvation Army, Goodwill, or local charities.
How do estate sales work for probate in Orange County?
We work with Orange County probate attorneys regularly. We can provide court-required documentation and fair market value appraisals.
