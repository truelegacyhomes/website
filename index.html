Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? See our complete FAQ or contact us.

How is an estate sale different than a garage sale?
An estate sale is basically a garage sale on steroids. Estate sales are used when you want to sell all (or almost all) of the contents of a home—typically when a loved one passes or you need to downsize quickly.
How many items do I need to have a sale?
There are NO item minimums or limits. Even if you only have five items, give us a call.
Is there a cost for care placement services?
No. Our services are free to families. We're paid by the care communities, allowing us to offer unbiased guidance at no charge to you.
Do I need to make repairs before selling my home?
No. We buy homes in any condition—as-is. Whether the house needs updates or a full cleanout, we handle it after closing.
After the estate sale, is my home completely empty?
Yes. We use trash removal services and partnerships with the Salvation Army to donate remaining items. Your home will be completely empty and left broom clean.
