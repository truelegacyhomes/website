Common Questions About Estate Sales in San Diego

How much does an estate sale cost?

Most San Diego estate sale companies (including us) work on commission—typically 30-40% of sales. You pay nothing upfront. We only get paid when items sell, so we're motivated to get you the best prices.

How long does the whole process take?

Typically 2-3 weeks from our first meeting to sale day. We handle sorting, pricing, staging, advertising, running the sale, and cleanup. For urgent situations (lease ending, closing date), we can sometimes work faster.

What if I live out of state?

No problem—many of our clients are settling estates for parents who lived in San Diego. We can handle everything remotely with video walkthroughs, digital photo updates, and secure payment processing.

What happens to items that don't sell?

We coordinate donation pickup (tax-deductible receipts provided) and can arrange junk removal for anything left over. You get a clean, empty house ready for sale or turnover.

Can you help with care placement AND an estate sale?

Yes! This is actually our specialty. We can help find the right assisted living community, then coordinate the estate sale to cover costs and timeline. Many families need both—we make it seamless.
