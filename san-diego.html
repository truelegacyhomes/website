San Diego Estate Sale FAQs

How much does an estate sale cost in San Diego?
Most San Diego estate sale companies charge 30-40% commission. True Legacy Homes works on commission only—no upfront fees. You don't pay unless we sell.
How long does it take to prepare for an estate sale?
Typically 1-2 weeks. We handle all staging, pricing, and marketing so you don't have to.
What items sell best at San Diego estate sales?
San Diego buyers love: mid-century modern furniture, coastal decor, surf memorabilia, vintage California art, fine jewelry, outdoor patio furniture, and quality tools/garage items.
Do I need to be present during the estate sale?
No. Most clients prefer to be away during the sale. We handle everything and provide detailed reporting through our digital point-of-sale system.
What happens to items that don't sell?
We arrange donation pickup or disposal. Many San Diego clients donate remaining items to Salvation Army, Goodwill, or local charities. Your home will be left broom-clean.
How do estate sales work for probate in San Diego?
We work with San Diego probate attorneys regularly. We can provide court-required documentation and fair market value appraisals for the probate process.
